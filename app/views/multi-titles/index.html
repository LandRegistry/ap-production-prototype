Multi titles

This feature enables a caseworker to process DRS single charge multi titles (up to 25 titles) in Application Processing. The linked demo represents the minimum viable product (MVP) build.

This demo includes:

  • An application with 3 titles (address, land to the back of and associated garage) submitted for private individual borrowers.
  • A subsequent discharge on the first/ lead title which will be automated. Subsequent discharge is the only additional transaction enabled for this feature.
  • An additional unecessary correspondence document will form part of the application to display the placement of the document retention task when not automated.

As the first/ lead title contains a subsequent discharge and charge transaction, the 'combinations framework' will be used and 'initial tasks' pages will also display. This feature will be iterated to reduce task repetition in later phases.
